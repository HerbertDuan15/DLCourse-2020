# Cat&DogClassification
## ./data 
------>train.txt: 1) Training set. Contains about 2W Chinese movie reviews, including 1W for positive and negative reviews.
------>validation.txt: 2) Verification set. Contains about 6K Chinese movie reviews, including about 3K positive and negative reviews.
------>test.txt: 3) Test set. It contains about 360 Chinese movie reviews, including about 180 positive and negative reviews.
------>wiki_word2vec_50.bin: 4) Pre-training word vectors. Chinese Wikipedia word vector word2vec.
## train_and_test.py
